Reading Is Fun!

Reading isn't just educational , or as boring as you might think it is. It's actually pretty fun !

The Beginning of Your Fun Filled Reading Experience

To like a book is nice. But to enjoy and want to read it is amazing. To enjoy a book, you have to find a genre that interests you. Once you have found an interesting genre that suits you, find books of that genre. Once found, try to read that book everyday or atlest once a week. If you are interested in that book, try finding more like it. Here are some examples:

How Reading Helps Your Everyday Life

Reading helps your everyday life by, for example, strengthening your reading comprehension level greatly, which will make you smarter, which will help you get through your life easier.

Reading can improve your writing skills, and it can also improve your memory skills too.

Reading is way healthier than playing video games. When you play video games, your brain doesn't get as much intellegence as you would get reading. But when you read, you are not only getting smarter, your writing skills improve too. When you read (and aren't playing video games or any other electronic), your writing skills will increase. Most authors will have writing technics, not like anybody elses. It's kinda like thier own style. Some authors writing technics, for example, is funny or serious. When you see other authors writing, you kind of make your own.

Learn to Love Reading

The First Step

The first step to learning to love reading is, as mentioned earlier, to find an interesting genre that you like and finding books of that genre. There are many genres to choose from. Here are some genres to get you started on your journey:

  • Fiction
  • Fantasy
  • Fairy Tales
  • Historical Fiction
  • Science Fiction
  • Non-Fiction
  • Autobiography
  • Biography
  • Informational Writing
  • Persuasive Writing
  • Tall Tale
  • Romance
  • Adventure
  • Horror
  • Folklore

Once you've picked a genre, as stated earlier, find books of your genre of choice, and try to read them atleast almost every day. That can lead to a liking, maybe evan a loving of reading.
